Founder & Director

GURU VIDUSHI SHWETHA MANJUNATHA BILGIKAR

Exquisite Classical Dancer, Choreographer & Guru

Miss. Shwetha is an exquisite classical dancer with instinctive abhinaya and excellent nrutha, who has dazed audiences with her scintillating performances across India. She has imbibed the nuances of Bharatanatyam under the expert tutelage of Guru Nrutya Vidushi Seema Bhagwath. In addition to Bharatanatyam, she has gained stellar proficiency in Kuchipudi and Kathak.

Recognized as a young, thriving, and highly esteemed guru, Shwetha is fondly nurturing a large family of aspiring students through the trust. She has performed extensively at prestigious national festivals including Kadambotsava, Karavali Utsava, and Sharavati Utsava, lighting up stages in Bangalore, Pune, Chennai, Hyderabad, and beyond.

As an acclaimed choreographer, she has conceptualized multiple complex dance ballets (dance dramas) synthesis of various traditional Indian classical styles. Her profound dedication has earned her wide appreciation from art lovers alongside many state-level and national honors.
